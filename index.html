Welcome!

to the UM BFA (Honours) Digital Arts Online Exhibition 2022

This online exhibition was launched in conjunction with the physical exhibition being held at the Department of Media and Knowledge Sciences (MaKS). The exhibition is a culmination of works created by the digital art students for their Final Year Project and no two projects are the same. Each project is something that the artist is very passionate about and as such has put a lot of research, time and effort in. All done in order to deliver something that is as beautiful as it is brilliant.
